## Copywriting Guidelines

### Category Headlines
**WRONG:** "The Best Clumping Cat Litters of 2024"
**RIGHT:** "We Tested 87 Clumping Litters. Here Are the Only 8 Worth Buying."

### Product Descriptions (Grid)
Keep it to 2-3 sentences maximum:
```
WRONG:
"This is an excellent clumping litter with great odor control and minimal dust. It's a premium option that delivers superior performance."

RIGHT:
"The best dust-free clumping we tested. You'll pay 30% more but get rock-solid clumps and zero visible dust. Worth it if dust bothers you."
```

### Quick Take Format
```
Best for: [Specific use case]
Worth buying if: [Specific criteria]
Skip it if: [Specific criteria]
Cost: [$/day calculation]
```

### Comparison Table Language
Use objective measurements and clear winners:
- "9/10 (Best in class)" not just "9/10"
- "âœ“ Flushable" not "Yes"
- "$0.51/day" not just "$24.99"

## Category-Specific Considerations

### Best Clumping
Focus on: Clump strength, dust levels, longevity
Pain points: Dust clouds, crumbly clumps, frequent replacement

### Best Natural
Focus on: Biodegradability, flushability, eco-impact
Pain points: Price premium, performance doubts, greenwashing

### Best Budget
Focus on: Value equation, performance per dollar
Pain points: "Cheap means bad", dust, poor clumping

### Best Odor Control
Focus on: Ammonia neutralization data, multi-cat performance
Pain points: Just masking odor, fragrance overload

### Best Low-Dust
Focus on: Particulate measurements, allergy concerns
Pain points: "Low dust" marketing lies, health issues

## Interactive Features

### Filter System
- Real-time filtering (no page reload)
- Show result count as filters change
- "Clear all filters" button
- Persist filters in URL (shareable filtered results)

### Sort Options
- Remember user's last sort preference
- Visual indicator of current sort
- Smooth transition when changing

### Compare Checkbox
- Max 4 products for comparison
- Persistent bar at bottom showing selected products
- "Compare Now" button appears when 2+ selected

## Responsive Design

### Mobile Considerations
- Quick Picks show 1 at a time (swipeable carousel)
- Filters collapse into drawer/modal
- Comparison table scrolls horizontally
- Product grid: 1 column on mobile, 2 on tablet, 3 on desktop
- Sticky "Compare" bar at bottom

### Desktop Enhancements
- Sticky filter sidebar
- Hover previews on product cards
- Quick-view modal (click product for overlay with details)
- Side-by-side comparison without leaving page
